Questions & Answers

What is the dress code?

We'd love to see our family and friends get dressed up with us! The dress code is semi-formal or cocktail attire. Think cocktail dresses, dressy jumpsuits, or suits and ties.

Can I bring a plus one?

Due to venue capacity, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation. If you received a plus one, they will appear under your name when you RSVP. Thank you for understanding!

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, our wedding is going to be an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time so you can celebrate with us.
